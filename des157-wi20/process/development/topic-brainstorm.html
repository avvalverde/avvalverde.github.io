Topic Brainstorm

At this point I would like to focus on developing an application or website that provides simple exercises that encourage mindfulness, emotionally restorative or encouraging reflections, and the ability to build confidence or a healthy sense of self-worth.

Introspection

I have struggled with depression since childhood and sought out evidence-based therapies (like cognitive behavioral therapy) and medication in order to manage the symptoms. At times they have been debilitating, and have certainly affected my quality of life.
